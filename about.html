Our Story

Exhale is a multi-author blog run by six graduates. Our biggest concern is providing mental health, as well as understanding mental disorders. Currently our team has expanded up to 25 members, and we keep gaining new members each year. Any one of our members can become an Exhale Expert, giving therapy sessions or running the conversation in the counseling forum. Exhale aims to provide available resources and fundamental facts about mental health. The biggest factor is that we don't judge. Exhale invites all individuals who are in need.

Meliza Riga - Director

34 | Indianapolis, IN

Meliza was diagnosed with an early onset of Schizophrenia at the age of 13. With professional help, as well as support from her family and friends, she worked hard to maintain a good mental stability. Riga developed Exhale in order to help those who urgently need proper care, along with a great, inviting atmosphere. She believes that people need an environment where they can feel safe and comfortable when it comes to mental health.

Levell Butler - Web Developer

22 | San Diego, CA

Levell Butler is the Graphic Designer and Web Developer of Exhale. Around college, Butler developed an extreme case of depression, affecting his design work negatively. He lacked any motivation to go to class and had extreme trouble interacting with others on campus. Developing Exhale was a major shift in Butler's life, as he not only wanted to help others with depression, but also provide some comfort for his own mental issue.

Roxane D'Aboville - Co-Director

30 | Patagonia, AZ

Roxane D'Aboville is a survivor of a tragic accident that happened in her family home back in 2008. She had a full diagnosis of PTSD and was triggered by loud noises, flashing lights, and certain phrases or keywords that reminded her of that night. After ten years, D'Aboville overcame her PTSD; she attended therapy, traveled around the world, and held PSA meetings about mental health. D'Aboville now provides her own therapy for those with PTSD and Paranoia. She is currently the partner of Meliza Riga and Co-Director of Exhale.
